About Us

Welcome to Seven Dyz Kids Botique

We Are the Best Kids Manufacturing Company In Kerala

2018 marked Seven Dyz Baby Care's first year in business. Born and raised in the outlying village of Perinthalmanna in the southern Indian state of Kerala's Malappuram district, Seven Dyz gradually rose to become a well-known name in children's clothing. Haris, a man with excellent business sense and creative ideas, is the creator of Seven Dyz. He nurtured the brand throughout its development and helped it to become the most sought-after brand in its category. A wonderful selection of baby outfits, from the magical one year to the naughty six, are available from Seven Dyz. Mothers who respect their child's happiness always choose Seven Dyz, and they refer that Seven Dyz is the most comfortable, hygienic, and reasonably priced children's clothing brand.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide safe, high-quality and comfortable clothing for babies, while promoting sustainable practices in our manufacturing process, and giving back to the community

OUR VISION

Seven Dys is to be the leading baby wear manufacturer in the industry, known for our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation. We aim to be a trusted and reliable brand that parents and caregivers turn to for all their baby wear needs. We envision a future where our products are available in a wide range of sizes and styles to suit every child's unique needs and preferences. We want to be a company that helps make parenting easier, more comfortable, and more enjoyable for families around the world.
